Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions

How do I book an appointment?
You can book an appointment by visiting our "Book Appointment" page, selecting your preferred doctor and specialty, choosing an available time slot, and confirming your booking. You'll receive a confirmation email with all the details.
What are your consultation hours?
Our doctors are available 24/7 for online consultations. For in-person visits, our office hours are Monday to Friday, 9:00 AM to 6:00 PM, and Saturday 9:00 AM to 2:00 PM. We're closed on Sundays and public holidays.
How much does a consultation cost?
Consultation fees vary by specialty and doctor. Video consultations typically range from $50-$150, while in-person consultations may cost more. You can see the exact price before booking. We also accept most major insurance plans.
Can I cancel or reschedule my appointment?
Yes, you can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours before the scheduled time without any charges. To do so, log in to your account, go to "My Appointments," and select the reschedule or cancel option.
Is my medical information secure?
Absolutely! We take your privacy seriously and comply with HIPAA regulations. All your medical records and personal information are encrypted and stored securely. We never share your information without your explicit consent.
Do you offer emergency services?
For life-threatening emergencies, please call 911 immediately. For urgent medical concerns that aren't life-threatening, we offer same-day video consultations with available doctors. You can also use our emergency consultation feature for immediate assistance.
